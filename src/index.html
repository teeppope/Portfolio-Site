About me

With a background in design, and an interest in finding the intersection of art and science, I was driven to explore web development as a form of digital crafting.

Over my seven-year career, I’ve worked alongside talented designers and project managers to provide engaging, vivid online experiences for clients across the hospitality, tourism, healthcare, and technology industries. Through these experiences I’ve honed an empathetic and compassionate approach to web development with a strong focus in web accessibility, site performance and end user experience.

Driven by curiosity, I continually explore new advancements in web development, while also expanding my abilities in sewing and crocheting.

more about me

I've always been fascinated by the intersection of art and science, which was sparked by my exposure to the digital world through platforms like Neopets and summers spent playing video games like Mario Kart.

After completing my Diploma in Graphic Communications and Print Design from the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology, I relocated to Vancouver to work as a UI Designer at a web design and development company. It was there that I discovered my passion for coding and transforming designs into interactive experiences.

In 2016, I transitioned from a designer to a developer by attending the Web Development bootcamp at Red Academy, where the course covered HTML, CSS, Javascript, Wordpress, PHP, web accessibility, Angular/React, build tools, and Git.

I have 7 years of experience working freelance and at digital agencies, building and maintaining websites for clients in various industries such as hospitality, tourism, healthcare, and technology. I have collaborated closely with designers, project managers, and clients, actively participating in all project stages and implementing an organized approach to the technical process. My expertise lies in web accessibility, performance optimization, and delivering engaging user experiences. I am proficient in working with Wordpress, Shopify, Squarespace, and React projects.

Recently, I have been exploring static site generators, furthering my knowledge of web accessibility, and expanding my skills in sewing and crocheting.

a selection of Web projects

Bushtops Safari Camps built at Wallop

  • Wordpress
  • ACF
  • Twig
  • SASS
  • JS

Bushtops Camps is a luxury safari hospitality brand based in Kenya and Tanzania. We developed their new website on Wordpress, aligning it with their updated branding. The site provides streamlined information for guests, with engaging animations and thoughtful design elements to enhance the user experience. As a unique feature, we created a custom Migration Map using the Google Maps API and wildebeest migration data to highlight the wildebeest migration's timing at each Bushtops camp.

Visit the Bushtops Camps site

Daanaa built at Massive Media

  • Wordpress
  • Gutenberg
  • ACF
  • PHP
  • SASS
  • JS

Daanaa is a Vancouver-based engineering startup specializing in innovative energy conduction and storage methods. We delivered an outstanding user experience by prioritizing web accessibility and a flexible authoring experience using Wordpress’ Gutenberg editor. The design and development approach we took elevated their brand, clearly communicating the company’s proprietary technology through the use of CSS animations and a unique accordion component that blends illustrated videos and written explanations, complete with auto play and pause functionality.

Visit the Daanaa site

Zingerman's Cornman Farms built at Wallop

  • Wordpress
  • ACF
  • Twig
  • SASS
  • JS
  • Shopify

Cornman Farms is a culinary and event venue in Dexter, Michigan, affiliated with Zingerman's Community of Businesses. We developed a new website for them that captured the essence of their farm experience with site-wide styles that evoke a scrapbook design aesthetic, including custom button shapes, animated SVG illustrations, and paper texture SVG edges. We implemented a custom wedding filter form for easy selection of suitable wedding options based on budget and needs. After the main site launched, we restyled a Shopify theme to align with their main site, enabling them to sell their culinary experiences.
