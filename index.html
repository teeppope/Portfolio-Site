About

I'm a Vancouver, CA based front-end web developer with a focus in building enjoyable user experiences relating to animations, speed and accessibility. Over the past 5 years I've worked in various agency settings along with the occasional freelance web project. Agency work and freelancing have granted me the opportunities to work with Wordpress, HTML, CSS, JS and even dabble with Shopify, Laravel and React/Gatsby.
