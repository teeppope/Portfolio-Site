About

the short bio of Terra

I came to web development with a background in design while following my curiosity to learn how the digital world is built. I see web development as digital crafting. Using various coding language and building an interactive online experience to help users achieve their goals, is just so cool and important.

I’ve been working as a Front End Developer for 7 years in agency and freelance capacities and along the way have established a development approach based on kindness, compassion and empathy for all users. I structure project code with a focus on digital accessibility, site performance and user experience utilizing the latest HTML, CSS, and Javascript techniques.

the long bio of Terra

Growing up I was always drawn to the intersection of art and sciences. The birth of the internet and video games introduced me to the digital world. Sites like Neopets, and summers spent playing Mario Kart, opened my eyes to a way art and science could be combined in creative ways.

After high school I turned my eye towards learning Graphic Design, receiving a Diploma in Graphic Communications and Print Design from the Southern Alberta Institute of Technology and moved from Calgary to Vancouver to pursue a job as a UI Designer at a web design and development company with two close friends. Working alongside these friends showed me the world of coding and really sparked my interest in building the digital world, and the creativity that goes into turning designs into an interactive reality.

In the Summer of 2016, after working for a year as an Office Manager at Skyrocket Digital, I attended the Web Development bootcamp course at Red Academy in Vancouver to switch gears from designer to developer. This course involved learning the ins and outs of HTML, CSS, Javascript, Wordpress, PHP, web accessibility and introduced me to Angular/React, build tools, github and some really great people.

For the past 7 years I’ve worked freelance and at digital agencies building and maintaining websites for clients in the hospitality, tourism, healthcare, and technology industries. Working primarily on Wordpress websites, I’ve focussed on web accessibility, performance and building enjoyable user experiences. Working closely with designers, project managers and clients, I’ve been actively involved in all stages of a projects life cycle providing an organized approach to the technical process. I’ve also gotten the opportunity to work on Shopfiy, Squarespace, and React projects over the years.

Lately I’ve been dabbling with static site generators, continuing to expand my web accessibility knowledge and growing my skillsets in the physical world through sewing clothing and crocheting.

some of My fave web projects

Bushtops Safari Camps built at Wallop

Bushtops Camps is a collection of luxury safari destinations in Kenya and Tanzania run by the Stuart Family. In late 2019 they rebranded, requiring a newly designed and built website to showcase the new branding. We built the new website on Wordpress, creating a new custom theme built with Twig templating, Sass, and Vanilla JS.

  • Implemented the newly branded design system through the creation of reuse-able flexible content modules using Advanced Custom Fields for an easy authoring experience
  • Maintained site Accessibility throughout the content modules through the use of conditional logic within the Twig templating language
  • Utilized the Google Maps API and wildebeest migration data to create a Wildebeest Migration Map to showcase where the migration overlaps with Bushtops camp locations throughout the year
Visit the Bushtops Camps site

Daanaa built at Massive Media

Daanaa is an engineering startup based in Vancouver, BC working to develop the next in energy conduction and storage. It’s proprietary technology required a clear, but intriguing brand with an interactive website to properly portray the startups vision. Their website is a fully custom Wordpress theme, built using Php, Sass, and Vanilla JS.

  • Delivered a flexible authoring experience for the client with accessibility top of mind, providing an exceptional user experience on the front end
  • Used simple css animations throughout the site to elevate and highlight the company’s new brand
  • Showcased the company’s proprietary technology by blending illustrated videos and written explanations into an accordion component that auto plays and pauses the videos
Visit the Daanaa site

Zingerman's Cornman Farms built at Wallop

Cornman Farms is a culinary, event and wedding venue in Dexter, Michigan — just ten miles northwest of Ann Arbor where its parent company, Zingerman’s Community of Businesses, is based. They were looking for a new website to better represent the Cornman Farms experience. We built them a custom Wordpress theme with Twig templating, Sass and Vanilla JS.

  • Implemented site wide styles to adhere to the scrapbook design feel through the use of custom button shapes, animated SVG illustrations and paper texture SVG edges.
  • Created a custom wedding filter form to let users quickly see which wedding type offered would suit their budget and needs.
  • After the main site launch we re-styled a Shopify theme to match their original site, so they could sell their culinary experiences
